## What you will make

You're going to build a **pizza clicker**: a game where you tap a pizza to make
more pizzas, spend those pizzas on shiny equipment so every click is worth more,
and hire helpers who keep cooking even when you take a break. Games like this are
called **idle** or **endless clicker** games, and the fun comes from watching your
numbers climb faster and faster.
